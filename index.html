About Me

Chang Sik Lee is an experienced electrical engineer with a focus on AI, robotics, and control systems. He earned his M.S. in Electrical Engineering from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) and is currently a researcher at the Korea Institute of Industrial Technology (KITECH), AI&Mobility Lab, Robotics R&D Department, Research Institute of Convergence Technology. His research interests include applications of machine learning, few-shot learning, and autonomous drone systems.
