About Me

I am Chang Sik Lee, an AI and Robotics researcher. My research interests include applications of machine learning, few-shot learning, and autonomous drone systems. I have a Master's degree in Electrical Engineering from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) and a Bachelor's degree in Electronic and Electrical Engineering from Sungkyunkwan University.
