About Me

Chang Sik Lee is an experienced electrical engineer with a focus on robotics, AI, and control systems. He earned his Ph.D. from the Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST) and is currently a senior researcher at the Korea Institute of Machinery & Materials (KIMM). His research interests include deep reinforcement learning, model predictive control, satellite attitude control, and hyperspectral imaging.
